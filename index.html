How to Play

The objective of Munch Down is to collect as many apples as possible, increasing your score and worms length. If you run into your body, you will explode and respawn

Timed Mode

In this game mode you have a 60 second timer, the goal is to get the highest score possible in the limited time

Free 4 All

In this game mode, there are no restrictions, get the highest score, but make sure not to run into yourself!
